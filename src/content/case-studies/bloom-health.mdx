## The Brief

Bloom Health had a problem most startups would envy: a major retail partnership lined up, contingent on having a brand and digital presence ready in five weeks.

They had a product — a personalized supplement subscription aimed at women over 35 — and a founding team with deep expertise in nutrition science. What they didn't have was any brand identity, a website, or a clear sense of how to talk to their customer.

Five weeks is not a lot of time. We had to move fast without cutting corners.

## The Approach

We used our Launch Package framework: a compressed, sequential sprint where strategy feeds directly into design, and design feeds directly into build. No waiting for approvals. No scope creep. One team, one timeline.

Week one was strategy only. We interviewed the founders, mapped the competitive landscape, and identified the positioning gap: most supplement brands talk about ingredients. Bloom's real story was about the relationship between nutrition science and the specific hormonal shifts women experience in their thirties and forties. That's a harder story to tell — and a much more defensible one.

Weeks two and three: visual identity. Logo, color system, typography, packaging direction. We made decisions fast and defended them clearly, which kept feedback loops short.

Weeks four and five: the site. Four pages — home, product, about, and a waitlist landing page — built on top of the brand strategy. Every headline tested against the positioning. Every section with a clear job to do.

## The Result

Bloom launched on schedule, with the retail partnership secured.

In the first week, 400 people signed up for the pre-launch waitlist without any paid media — organic referrals only. The founding team used the brand deck we created in every subsequent investor and partnership meeting.

They told us the strategy work paid for itself in the first conversation it changed.

> "We had five weeks and needed everything. Lily Pad gave us a brand that felt like it had been years in the making."

— Priya S., Co-Founder, Bloom Health
