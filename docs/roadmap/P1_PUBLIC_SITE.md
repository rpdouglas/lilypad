**Status:** 🔄 Active

**Started:** —

**Primary Personas:** Sarah (mobile-first, fast decisions, social proof above the fold), Marcus (desktop, needs depth and process proof)

## Goal

Home, Services, About, and Contact/Booking pages — mobile-responsive, persona-validated, deployed to production domain with SSL. The site must earn trust fast enough that Sarah books a call and give Marcus enough proof to justify budget internally.

## Deliverables

### Home (`/`)

- [ ] Hero — one confident headline (transformation, not service list), single CTA: "Start a Project"
- [ ] Proof strip — client logos or impact bar ("14 brands launched") above the fold
- [ ] Problem framing section — makes the visitor feel seen before the pitch
- [ ] Services summary — 3–4 outcome cards linking to `/services`
- [ ] Featured case study teaser — problem → approach → result with client quote
- [ ] Process preview — 3–5 step visual of the Lily Pad Launch method
- [ ] Testimonials — 2–3 with photo (video in P5)
- [ ] Insights teaser — latest 2–3 posts (placeholder until P2)
- [ ] Final CTA — full-width block, single action: "Book a discovery call"

### Services (`/services`)

- [ ] Services overview page
- [ ] Brand Strategy sub-page
- [ ] Web Development sub-page
- [ ] Design Systems sub-page
- [ ] Launch Packages sub-page

### About (`/about`)

- [ ] Story and values
- [ ] Team section
- [ ] Press / partnerships placeholder (content in P5)

### Contact & Booking (`/start`)

- [ ] Cal.com embed — direct calendar, zero friction
- [ ] Single purpose page — no nav distractions

### Foundation

- [ ] Mobile-responsive across all pages (test on 375px, 768px, 1280px)
- [ ] Brand tokens applied consistently — forest, coral, cream, stone, dark
- [ ] Typography — Cormorant Garamond (display), Outfit (body), DM Mono (labels)
- [ ] Framer Motion page transitions wired
- [ ] Deployed to production domain with SSL

## Persona Gate

Before closing this phase, answer all three:

1. **Sarah:** Would she understand the hero headline in under 10 seconds on mobile? Is the CTA visible without scrolling?
2. **Marcus:** Does the Services page give him enough process depth to justify the budget internally? Is there a clear defined methodology?
3. **Dana:** Not a target of this phase — portal is P3.

Run `/testing` before marking this phase done.
