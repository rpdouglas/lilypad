# Persona: Sarah — The Founder

## Role
Startup Founder, Series A

## Archetype
The Founder

## Goals
- Launch a credible brand fast
- Look like a $10M company with a $50K budget
- Move quickly — time is the scarcest resource at this stage

## Fears
- Wasting money on a beautiful site that doesn't convert
- Inconsistency across channels undermining credibility
- Getting locked into a slow agency process that delays launch

## Behaviors
- Compares 3–5 firms before deciding
- Reads case studies obsessively — looking for proof of ROI, not aesthetics
- Books calls from her phone at 10pm after the kids are asleep
- Makes decisions fast once trust is established
- Shares links with co-founders via iMessage for a quick gut-check

## Key Pages
- Home → Work → /start

## Device & Context
- Primary: Mobile (iPhone, evening hours)
- Secondary: Desktop (quick research during the day)

## Design Checklist Question
> "Would Sarah understand this in under 10 seconds?"

## Implications for Build
- Every CTA must be thumb-friendly (min 44px touch target)
- Hero headline must communicate the transformation instantly — no clever wordplay
- Case studies must lead with outcomes and numbers, not process
- The path from landing to booked call must take under 3 minutes on mobile
- No long forms — friction kills conversion for this persona
