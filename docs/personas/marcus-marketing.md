# Persona: Marcus — The Marketing Director

## Role
Marketing Director, Mid-Market Company

## Archetype
The Marketing Director

## Goals
- Find a firm that can handle strategy AND execution — not just one or the other
- Build a business case to justify budget to his C-suite before the first call
- Establish a reliable, process-driven engagement with clear milestones
- Avoid scope creep and missed deadlines that will reflect badly on him internally

## Fears
- Agencies that claim to be "full-service" but are mediocre at everything
- Scope creep eroding the budget he fought to get approved
- Missed deadlines that damage his credibility with leadership
- Paying for strategy that never gets executed

## Behaviors
- Reads the Insights blog to assess expertise before reaching out
- Wants to see a defined process and methodology — not just a portfolio
- Needs proposals with clear deliverables and timelines, not open-ended discovery
- Browses on desktop during business hours
- Will share the site URL with his VP or CMO for sign-off before booking

## Key Pages
- Services → Insights → About

## Device & Context
- Primary: Desktop (during business hours)
- Secondary: Mobile (occasional quick checks)

## Design Checklist Question
> "Does Marcus have enough proof here to justify budget internally?"

## Implications for Build
- Services page must show a defined process with clear phases and timelines
- Pricing signal is essential — even a "Starts at $X" range lets him pre-validate with leadership
- Case studies must include quantified outcomes ("40% reduction in review cycles")
- The About page needs to establish credibility through press, awards, and third-party validation
- FAQ on the Services page should address scope, timelines, and revision process directly
- Insights blog (Phase 2) is a primary trust-builder for this persona
